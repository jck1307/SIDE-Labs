SIDE Feedback Terms of Use

This document describes how BlueXML will treat the data provided by individuals and organizations through the SIDE Feedback.

  • A unique id is generated for every workstation that submits data using the SIDE Feedback. This session id is transmitted every time data is sent to BlueXML.
  • BlueXML does not intend for the SIDE Feedback to collect information that can uniquely identify you as an individual.
  • The data that is being collected relates to how you are using SIDE. This includes, but is not limited to, information on generators and deployers names, version numbers, options used.

Uses of the Information

  • BlueXML may, in its sole discretion, make available to organizations and individuals, on a case-by case-basis, the data that it collects through the SIDE Feedback, whether in raw or aggregated form.
  • BlueXML will publish summary reports based on the data obtained. These reports will be made available in machine readable format that will allow individuals and organizations to undertake further analysis.

Interactions with the SIDE Feedback

  • With your permission, the SIDE Feedback will periodically transmit your usage data to the Eclipse Foundation. The first time it attempts to transmit the data, you will be requested to grant permission of transmission. If you decline the request, the SIDE Feedback will stop functioning, until you decide to re-enable it. If you grant permission, you will have the option of allowing the SIDE Feedback to automatically transmit data without further interaction or to request your permission each time it attempts to transmit data.
  • At anytime, you may view the data that is being collected. To view your current information, go to the "Usage Data Collection | Preview" preferences page.
  • After providing your initial permission, you may discontinue use of the SIDE Feedback by turning off the "Change Upload Preference" on the "Usage Data Collection" preferences page.

More Information
